How to Play


This is a Hide and Seek inspired card game!

You are the HIDER.

The goal is to survive 10 rounds without being caught by the SEEKER.

Use the Hide button to hide! But, be careful, you can only hide 2 consecutive turns.

Use the Play button to play a random card from your deck.

If Seeker Attack! and you are not hidden, you lose!

Good luck!
